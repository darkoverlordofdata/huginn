Don't you just love to compare the size of things to the Space Needle? I know I do. For example, at 328 feet, Godzilla is 1.84 Space Needles tall. We better hope Godzilla doesn't come to Seattle.

So, the question them is:

How many space needles tall are you?

Me? I am .0099 Space Needles tall, and yet I weight a scant .00004 Space Needles.

 

btw - the Space Needle is 605 ft tall and weighs 9,550 tons.
